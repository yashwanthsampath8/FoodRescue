About Food Rescue

Food Rescue India is a not-for-profit social organization working to solve hunger, malnutrition and food wastage in the country. We channelize extra food from individuals, weddings, restaurants, corporate offices to the people who really need it and have no means or access to food. We also serve freshly cooked food or raw grains to our partnered shelter homes and beneficiaries.

How Food Rescue works

1) Food Donors register themselves to the Food Rescue india application and login to the system.
2) Food Donors enter the necessary information in the food rescue details section.
3) Our admin notifies the particular location to our volenteer.
4) Voleneteer visits and collects the food and he will donate to the nearby social organisation partners.
